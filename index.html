Projects

Health Effects of Secondhand Cigarette Smoke Exposure in Multi-unit Housing [ongoing]

Led the project from conception as part of my Master’s thesis, including questionnaire design, participant recruitment, and data collection (survey data, pulmonary function test, blood pressure measurement, urine sample, measurement of particulate matter - PM1, PM2.5, PM10 concentrations). Processed and analyzed urine samples for urinary cotinine and trace heavy metals in a BSL-2 laboratory setting to assess the effects of secondhand smoke exposure on the collected health outcomes.

Public Knowledge, Attitudes and Perceptions of Air Quality [ongoing]

Contributed to securing ethics approval, designing of questionniare incorporating a standardized Air Quality Knowledge Index (AQ-IQ), and leading its implementation, deployment, and data collection on Qualtrics across the top 10 populous countries globally with over 10,000 respondents. Matched population-weighted PM2.5 concentration levels to the administrative units of each respondents’ residences across the 10 countries for exposure assessment using R. The open-source data processed for this project can be accessed in this repository for pop-weighted PM2.5 for countries.

OAC project [ongoing]

Contributed to securing ethics approval, designing and implementing a survey, and deploying it on Qualtrics for data collection. Processed and analyzed public's feedback and Volatile Organic Compound (VOC) data from the National Environment Agency and created interactive visualization maps using the leaflet package in R.

Home Air Quality Monitoring

Installed low-cost air sensors in the homes of elderly participants to track home air quality, recording data every second for approximately 14 days. Processed the recorded data using R and provided individualized reports online either in Chinese or English to participants who requested them. Reports are accessible in this repository for home air quality reports.

Health Effects of Cooking Fumes Exposure

Supported this pilot study through participant recruitment (hawker stall operators and office workers), field deployment, and data collection on nasal microbiome, field surveys, and personal air quality exposure. Installed Rooti RX electrocardiogram (ECG) devices on participants’ chests to collect per-minute ECG data for approxiately 7 days on cardiovascular health, and processed the data using R for preliminary analysis.

Harmonization of Metabolomics Data Across Cohorts

Conducted the harmonization and pooled analysis of metabolomics data from approximately 20 international cohorts within the COnsortium of METabolomics Studies (COMETS). Metabolites / lipids were harmonized acorss different databases such as HMDB, Metabolomics Workbench, PheWeb, ChemSpider, and PubChem. The harmonized data were then cross-checked using Excel VLOOKUP against the COMETS harmonization master list for consistency. Pooled analysis was conducted in R to examine the association of blood metabolomics and lung cancer risk and the identification of smoking-related metabolites.

Estimating Proximity of Public Housing to Nature Parks in Singapore

This geospatial project calculates the distance (in meters) from public residential buildings to the nearest nature parks in Singapore using open-source datasets and processed in R. the study employs the SVY21 coordinate system and incorporates spatial joins and geospatial calculations to provide proximity measurements for each residential building. The processed data is accessible and ready for download in this distance to park repository.
